# Masking Temple (org. Masking Tape)
Game about explorer of old thumb whit hold many secrets and many mysteries. 
And ancient Aztec artefact which which can uncover whole new world for you.


## Controls:
W, A, S, D - Movement

E - Interactions

Space - Jump
